About Me

I have an extensive background in both education and engineering. In terms of education, I have electrical engineering, computer science, and systems engineering degrees, in addition to certifications in education and mathematics.

Subjects

I tutor a variety of subjects, including test preparation for standardized tests such as SAT, STAAR, and ISEE.

From elementary up to basic high school level, I tutor all subjects, including all four GED subjects. For higher level classes, such as AP or college level, I focus on Math, Physical Sciences, and Computer Science (concepts and programming).
